Mgt of Water Jetting Checklist

to be used to plan every jetting operation:

Required PPE: Appropriate work wear, class 3 (long sleeve) high-vis, safety footwear, hard hat, full face shield or goggles with face mask, gloves, ear defenders (where necessary). Where large amounts of aerosols are likely to be generated wear respiratory protective equipment (RPE) fitted with a P3 filter
CLOSE DOWN - Don't forget to:
1. Store equipment correctly (antifreeze where required)
2. Leave site in a safe and tidy condition - all access covers replaced etc.

If impact from water jet occurs - seek medical attention as soon as possible.Take your HPWJ emergency card with you.
